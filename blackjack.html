Black Jack

Rules

  1. The aim of the game is to obtain a hand with a sum as close to 21 as possible without getting 21 or higher.
  2. When the game begins, each player will be dealt 2 cards to start with.
  3. If you select 'Hit Me!' a new card will be drawn and added to your hand.
  4. If you select 'Stand' you cannot draw a new card until the end of the round.
  5. At the end of the round, the player with the hand closest to 21 wins.
